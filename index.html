Teacher!!!

Teachers are a valuable cornerstone of every society and have perhaps the most profound impact on the youth and therefore the entire population later in their lives. As I may be interested in getting in on this, it is nescassary for me to do the proper research before investing too much physically or emotionally into pursuing this chase.

Overview:


Responsibility

Teachers are responsible for watching after students and making sure that they are on track with the lesson plan and comprehending the material.

Environment

Teachers are required to work around kids and other teachers as well as comply with the orders of the board of education as well as higher powers.

Impact

As stated previously, teachers are perhaps the most impactful profession on the face of the Earth, greatly influencing the youth.

How to get there:


Requirements

While it can vary depending on state, country or grade, most teachers will need a bachelors degree in education and/or the field you will be teaching or tutoring in.

Qualities

A teacher must have patience, the ability to be understanding and empathize with a students situation, as well as a true interest in the material or a desire to instruct.

Schools

Some of the best schools an aspiring teacher can attend include Vanderbilt, University of Texas, as well as the well known Ivy Leagues like Stanford or Harvard.

Is it worth it?:


Salary Range

From elementary to high school, teacher salaries can range from $38,000-$105,000.

Job Outlook

The fact that schools and demand for teachers are expected to increase in the coming years as well as teachers salaries increasing by 5% makes this job outlook pretty good.

Other

Salaries can vary drastically depending on what state, country or grade you are teaching.
